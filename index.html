Wallet

Wallet is a concept for a finance application that focuses heavily on data visualization for the end-user. It should tie together very nicely a wide variety of financial information that is relevant to the user and their financial goals. This was my first data-heavy illustration and I wanted to challenge myself by providing an abundance of data in a user-friendly manner.

Horizon

This greeter page is for a relaxation/zen application. The simplicity and cleanness are meant to reflect that. I apply some blur to create the illusion of a horizon, without the use of solid colours or lines. It's also an example of how 1D objects on a canvas can produce a 2.5D effect. Shapes, and how they work together, are powerful tools for design.

Bermeja

Information you need to display at a glance should depend on the product being served. This is a landing page + hotel indexing web app for a fictional tropical island-nation. The goal here is to invite the user to buy into the product with the use of a great first impression, via visuals, as well as providing the means to easily get started buying into what is being sold by providing relevant information at a glance. The slogan, option to explore more, sign-up/sign-in information are all made visible to the user through optimal placement of those elements.

Adopt a Paw

It can feel like the term 'minimalism' is a loaded catch-phrase used as a fad these days but, in design, it can be a powerful tool. Adopt-a-paw is an illustration for pet adoption organization and the end goal of this kind of design is to provide imagery with only the most relevant information and to a variety of users(those who may want to adopt an animal vs someone who wants to volunteer their services). It gets straight to the point.

Fiore

Fiore is a demonstration of the use of responsive design to provide the same amount of features and information across two different display sizes - desktop and mobile phones. Different display sizes provide different opportunities to present data and information in a palatable way to the end-user by applying proper UI and UX practices to maximize screen real estate.

Merch Store

With the rise of platforms like YouTube, Twitch, etc merch stores have become the main source of income for non-profits, creatives and content creators. As with all my designs, I wanted to demonstrate that less is more and that clever placement of elements and options are vital for providing a good user experience for the end-user - which encourages them to buy. Pricing, colour options, merchandise info are all available at a glance.

Gradients

Colours and design are as inseparable from each other as faith is to religion. In fact, colour without any other design elements like text, shapes, icons or textures can constitute design. Solid colours are rarely ever-present in nature. A sunset can have varying shades of blues, pinks and oranges. Gradients are a pillar of modern design and can significantly enhance the visual experience. These are some of my favourite subtle gradients.
