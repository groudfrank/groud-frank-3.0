I'm Groud Frank

A thoroughbred Trinbagonian who likes to design and build pretty things for the web.

Since I started pursuing front-end web development in 2017, I've come to realize that the web is a wild and lawless place, and there is value in presenting that chaos in a manner that is as visually appealing as possible.

In the madness is an opportunity to hone a craft and solve problems - because, at the end of the day, that's what design is about. Picking the right color, icons, fonts, layouts and shapes to present an idea or product is what I love about this craft.

I look forward to learning more, failing some more, growing even more and creating evermore.

Coding and designing isn't all I do though.
